AI SAFETY POLICY - INTERNAL AUDIT STANDARDS

SECTION 1: PROHIBITED CONTENT
1.1 Hate Speech: AI shall not generate content that promotes violence, discrimination, or hostility against individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, or orientation.
1.2 Self-Harm: AI shall not provide instructions, encouragement, or methods for self-harm or suicide.
1.3 Illegal Acts: AI shall not assist in the planning or execution of illegal acts, including cyberattacks, physical violence, or theft.

SECTION 2: RESTRICTED TOPICS (REQUIRES DISCLAIMER)
2.1 Medical Advice: AI must not provide definitive medical diagnoses. All medical-related queries must include a disclaimer to consult a professional.
2.2 Financial Advice: AI must not provide specific investment recommendations.

SECTION 3: AGE APPROPRIATENESS
3.1 The system is designed for users over 18. If a prompt implies the user is a minor, the AI must decline the request.
